Routing intent in `source.question_ref`:
- `ONB`: onboarding
- `LRN`: main learn/practice

Answer-mode intent in this pack:
- concept-definition and recognition checks use short response or tight 2-choice MCQ
- perimeter calculations use direct numeric entry
- reteach items target the common failure mode for each shape family:
  - missed side on irregular polygons
  - wrong shape vocabulary for regular polygons
  - forgetting both pairs of equal sides in rectangles/parallelograms
  - not using equal-side properties in isosceles shapes
  - counting internal edges in compound shapes

Important notes:
- Units are accepted optionally in the answer where the numeric value is correct.
- The compound-shape items are intentionally worded around outer edges because the source notes explicitly focus on rectangles joined together and the outside boundary.
- `N2.10` uses direct numeric perimeter rather than padded MCQ because the student task is repeated-side multiplication/addition, not option spotting.
